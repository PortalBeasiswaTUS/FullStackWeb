Question and Answer

The basic requirements include a completed application form, academic transcripts, recommendation letters, and a personal statement.
The selection process typically takes 4 to 6 weeks after the application deadline.
You need to prepare your academic transcripts, identification documents, recommendation letters, and any other documents specified in the application guide.
Scholarship funds are usually disbursed within one month after the final selection results are announced.
